what is Artifical Intelligence?

Artifical Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer scinece focused on creatinng machines capable of performing tasks that typically required, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI: Designed for specific tasks(e.g., virtual assisants like Siri).
  • General AI: A theorectical concept where machines possess human-like cognitive abilities.
